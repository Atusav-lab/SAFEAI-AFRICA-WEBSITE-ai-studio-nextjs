Core capabilities

Everything your field team needs, nothing they don't.

GPS Store Check-In

Real browser geolocation captures exact coordinates at check-in. Distance validation confirms the representative is physically at the store before the audit form unlocks.

Live geolocation

Live Sales Tracking

Every sale is recorded against the live product catalogue in your local currency. Cascaded filters let supervisors drill from representative to store to product in seconds.

Local currency · Real-time

Variance Reconciliation

Orders enter the warehouse queue as Pending. Representatives or supervisors record deliveries and the system computes the exact delta — partial, over, or under — with no manual work.

Orders · Deliveries · Delta

Shelf Photo Uploads

Field representatives attach live shelf photographs directly from the browser. Images upload to cloud storage and appear as thumbnails inside the supervisor review panel.

Cloud storage

Compliance Audits

Planogram checks, expiry verification, FIFO adherence, pricing compliance, and OOS flagging — all captured in a single structured form per visit, reviewable by supervisors.

FIFO · OOS · Planogram

PDF & CSV Exports

Generate formatted activity reports as PDF or export filtered data as CSV in one click. Date range, store, and representative filters apply across every export.

Reports · PDF · CSV
The workflow

A closed loop from field to boardroom.

01

Route Assignment

Supervisors schedule weekday routes — assigning each representative an ordered list of stores. The system filters to only the outlets that representative manages.

02

GPS Check-In & Audit

The representative arrives, the browser confirms their position, and the compliance form unlocks. SOH, OOS items, shelf share, and photos are all captured in one flow.

03

Sales & Orders

Daily sales are entered with automatic currency computation. If stock is low, the representative raises a warehouse order — logged as Pending and queued for dispatch.

04

Supervisor Review

Every submission surfaces in the supervisor dashboard. Reports are approved or flagged. Variance is reconciled. Audit logs capture every action with impact severity levels.

Platform modules

Six modules. One unified console.

01
Unified Dashboard

Role-adaptive KPI cards, sales trend charts, interactive map visit traces, and a real-time leaderboard for supervisors.

02
Store Visits & Itinerary

Dual-audience view: field reps see their route checklist, supervisors see the Reps Routing Progress Control Panel with live progress bars per representative.

03
Dynamic Inventory Logs

Stock-on-hand snapshots per store with colour-coded low-stock alerts, local currency projections, and instant CSV export with date-stamped filenames.

SOH Min Stock Alerts CSV Export
04
Supply Variance & Orders

Field representatives raise orders by product and quantity. Representatives or supervisors record deliveries and the platform computes the exact variance delta automatically — no spreadsheets, no manual reconciliation required.

Pending → Partial → Delivered Auto variance compute

Reports Builder + Admin Console

Multi-filter reports with PDF and CSV export. Plus a full admin suite: user and store management, product catalog, weekday route scheduling, supervisor comment loops, and a complete audit log trail with impact severity levels.
